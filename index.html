Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigation skills.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Camels

  • When camels drink water,they drink 40 gallons in one go !.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils in case of a sandstorm.
  • There are over 160 different words for 'Camel' alone in Arabic.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas have one of the longest lifespans among any insects,living up to 17 years.
  • Humans eat cicadas too.Many restaurants in the USA serve them.
  • More than 3000 different species of cicadas exist.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas spend 10-16 hours in a day eating,mostly bamboo which is critical to their diet.
  • Baby pandas when born, measure 15 cm, about the size of a pencil! .
  • Strangely, pandas like to lick copper and iron! .

Interesting Facts About Bears

  • Grizzly bears can remember the faces of other bears! .
  • The brown bear is the national animal of Finland .
  • A bear fought in the Polish Army in WW2. He carried shells to the frontline and was taught to salute.
